Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?
Lisa is based in Dayton, Ohio and primarily serves the local area. She's also open to traveling for the right event — just include your location in your inquiry and she'll let you know what's possible.
What types of events do you bartend?
Weddings are a specialty, but Lisa bartends all kinds of celebrations — private parties, showers, corporate events, and more. If you're not sure whether your event is a fit, just ask!
Do you bartend at private homes?
Residential events are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Lisa is happy to serve at larger homes that can comfortably accommodate your guest count and provide a safe, suitable space for bartending. Standard residential settings may not always be the best fit — share your venue details in your inquiry and she'll be upfront about whether she can make it work.
How does pricing work?
Pricing depends on your event — date, guest count, location, and duration all factor in. Fill out the inquiry form below and Lisa will get back to you with a personalized quote. No surprises, no pressure.
What is dry hire?
Dry hire means you purchase all the alcohol and Lisa provides the bartending service plus mixers, garnishes, ice, and equipment. It's a popular option for events because you stay in control of your beverage budget.
